Common fake job scams in India

  • Work from home data entry jobs paying ₹500-800 per hour
  • Jobs requiring registration fee or joining fee upfront
  • Recruiter contacting only via WhatsApp or Telegram
  • Jobs offering salary of ₹50,000+ per week for no experience
  • Fake Infosys, TCS, Wipro job offers sent on WhatsApp
  • Part-time jobs asking for Aadhaar and bank details upfront
  • Government job offers from fake ministry websites
