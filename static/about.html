About Fake Job Detector India

Free ML-powered protection for Indian job seekers

Why we built this

Every day, millions of Indians receive fake job offers on WhatsApp, Naukri, and LinkedIn. Scammers pose as Infosys, TCS, government ministries, and other trusted organizations. They ask for registration fees, Aadhaar numbers, and bank details. People lose thousands of rupees and their personal data to these scams.

We built Fake Job Detector India to give every job seeker a free, instant way to verify any job post before applying or paying anything.

How it works

Our Machine Learning model was trained on 96,674 real job postings from two datasets: the EMSCAD dataset (17,880 global job posts labeled fake or real) and the Naukri.com India dataset (78,794 real Indian job posts). We use a Random Forest classifier with TF-IDF text analysis to achieve 99.67% accuracy in detecting fake job posts.

On top of the ML model, we apply India-specific rules that detect common local scam patterns — registration fees, WhatsApp-only contact, unrealistic salary claims, and fake government job offers.
